Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01/03/2025

Your privacy is very important to us. To demonstrate our commitment to transparency and best practices, we have developed this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy outlines how Circuit Strategies ("the Company," "us," "we," or "our") collects, handles, and stores user information through our website and any related websites linked to this Privacy Policy (the "Website"). Please read it carefully to understand how we process your information.

Personal Information

Information We Collect: We may collect a range of information, including your name, contact details, and billing information ("Personal Information"), depending on whether you are a visitor to the Website ("Visitors") or a user of our services ("Members"). For example, Members will be asked to provide Personal Information such as name, address, telephone number, email address, and billing details.

How We Collect Personal Information: We collect information when you interact with us, such as registering for events, purchasing products or services, subscribing to newsletters, or contacting us with inquiries. We also collect information automatically via cookies and similar tracking technologies.

How We Use Personal Information: We may use Personal Information to:

  • Operate and provide access to the Website and its features.
  • Manage and facilitate your registration and use of our services.
  • Process orders for products or services.
  • Communicate with you about your account, security issues, or updates to the Website.
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Conduct research and analysis to improve our services.
  • Protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, and comply with legal obligations.

Disclosure of Personal Information: We may disclose Personal Information:

  • To employees, affiliates, or third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Website or delivering our services.
  • To business partners as part of joint promotions or service offerings.
  • In the event of a business transaction, such as a merger or sale.
  • To comply with legal requirements, enforce our terms, or protect our rights.

Obtaining Consent for SMS Communications

By providing your phone number and opting into our SMS communications, you consent to receive automated messages related to our services and promotions. Standard messaging rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying “STOP” to any SMS message. We strictly adhere to carrier SMS policies and ensure compliance with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA).

Note: We do not share phone numbers collected for SMS purposes with third parties.

Other Information

Other Information We May Collect: We may collect information that does not directly identify you, such as browser and device information, IP addresses, and data collected through cookies.

How We Use and Disclose Other Information: We may use and disclose non-identifying information for research, analysis, and to improve our services. If we combine Other Information with Personal Information, it will be treated as Personal Information.

We take reasonable measures to protect your Personal Information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

The Website is not intended for individuals under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18.

Contacting Us
